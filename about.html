About Us

We are Creating unforgettable travel experiences

The Perfect Road Riders, an indicative name in providing an uncompromised Staff Transportation Service in Major Cities in Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh to most reputed customers

Beginning as a small business entity in 1994 providing service to Industry Houses, we have started building our strength focusing to extend the services to Major Industrial Giants & Corporate Houses. Presently we operate more than 820 Vehicles in various combination of AC , Non AC, Maxi Cabs , Tempo Travelers to suit both Individuals and Industrial Needs with different Maintenance yards and Diesel Filling facilities in the vicinity of the customers’ plant location.Apart from this, different parking yards have also been made facilitated enabling us to move the vehicle in time from the starting location, there by transporting the customers staff in time without any operational constraints to them. This type of dedicated service made us a major stakeholder in the smooth operations of these corporate houses by ensuring their manpower reaches its destinations on-time, every time.
